What Derails the Plan
Three Events That Destroy Financial Plans
💀
Death
Sudden and unexpected. Your income stops immediately. Your family's lifestyle depends entirely on what you've planned, not what you intended to plan.
What's lost
All future income · Family lifestyle · Children's education · Spouse security
🦠
Disease
A critical illness doesn't kill you — it leaves you alive but unable to earn, with mounting medical bills. The financial impact outlasts the illness itself.
What's at risk
Treatment costs · Lost income (2-3 yrs) · Savings eroded · Retirement delayed
♿
Disability
Your greatest asset isn't your home or savings — it's your ability to earn. Permanent disability can eliminate that entirely, while your expenses remain unchanged.
The scale
Monthly income multiplied by years to retirement equals your true financial worth
